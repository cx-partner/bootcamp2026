**Webex Financial Group: Customer Service & Policy Guide**

**Section 1: Credit Card Management**

**1.1 Card Expiration and Renewal**

- **Expiration Date:** All Webex Financial credit cards are valid until
  the last day of the month shown on the front of the card (MM/YY).

- **Automatic Renewal:** A replacement card is automatically mailed to
  the customer's registered address 30 days before the current card
  expires.

- **Activation:** New cards can be activated via the mobile app,
  website, or by calling the automated activation line.

**1.2 Card Locking and Security**

- **Temporary Lock:** Customers can \"Freeze\" or \"Lock\" their card
  instantly if it is misplaced. This prevents new purchases while
  allowing recurring subscriptions and returns to process.

- **Unlocking:** Cards can be unlocked immediately once found through
  the secure \"Authentication Check\" with an agent or the mobile app.

- **Permanent Loss:** If a card is confirmed stolen or lost, it must be
  canceled, and a new card number will be issued.

**Section 2: Debt Collection & Payments**

**2.1 Maturity Dates and Balances**

- **Maturity Date:** This is the final date by which an outstanding
  balance must be settled to avoid late fees or impact on credit scores.

- **Outstanding Balance:** The total amount currently owed, including
  principal and any accrued interest.

**2.2 Payment Options**

- **Total Payment:** Settling the full outstanding amount in a single
  transaction.

- **Partial Payment:** Paying a portion of the debt. While this reduces
  the balance, interest may continue to accrue on the remaining amount.

- **NovaPay Integration:** All digital payments are processed
  through **NovaPay**. Customers receive a unique, secure URL via email
  to complete their transactions.

**Section 3: Fraud Management & Security**

**3.1 Identifying Fraud Scenarios**

Fraud is suspected if a customer reports:

- Transactions from vendors they do not recognize.

- Charges from cities or countries they have not visited.

- Duplicate charges for a single purchase.

- Small \"test\" charges (often \$1.00 or less) from unknown sources.

**3.2 Fraud Reporting Process**

- **Immediate Action:** Upon a report of a suspicious transaction, the
  AI Agent must record the Transaction ID, Amount, Vendor, Date, and
  City.

- **Escalation:** All fraud claims must be escalated to a **Human Fraud
  Specialist** immediately.

- **Account Protection:** The specialist will determine if the card
  needs to be permanently blocked and a dispute (chargeback) initiated.

**Section 4: General Account Services**

**4.1 Rewards and Cashback**

- **Cashback Balance:** Customers earn a percentage of every purchase
  back into their \"Cashback Account.\" This can be applied as a
  statement credit.

- **Rewards Tier:** Tiers (Silver, Gold, Platinum) are based on annual
  spend and provide varying levels of interest rate discounts and travel
  benefits.

**4.2 Transaction History**

- AI Agents can provide detailed transaction data for the **last 7
  days**. For older records, customers are directed to their monthly PDF
  statements available in the online portal.

**Section 5: Frequently Asked Questions (Q&A)**

**Q: Can I change my payment due date?** **A:** Yes, customers can
request a change to their monthly billing cycle once every 12 months,
provided the account is currently in good standing.

**Q: What happens if I miss my maturity date?** **A:** A late fee may be
applied to the account, and the interest rate may increase to the
\"Default Rate\" as specified in your cardholder agreement. We recommend
making at least a partial payment to show intent to pay.

**Q: Is the payment link you sent me via email secure?** **A:** Yes. The
link is generated through **NovaPay**, our secure payment partner. It is
encrypted and unique to your specific transaction session.

**Q: I see a charge I don\'t recognize, but it\'s only for \$0.99.
Should I worry?** **A:** Yes. Fraudsters often use small amounts to
\"test\" if a card is active. Please allow me to record those details
and transfer you to a specialist to secure your account.

**Q: How do I earn more cashback?** **A:** You can increase your
cashback earnings by using your Webex Financial card at our \"Preferred
Partners,\" which include major grocery chains and fuel stations.

**Q: Can Alex (the AI) lower my interest rate?** **A:** No. As an AI
Agent, I can provide information and facilitate payments, but interest
rate negotiations must be handled by our specialized Credit Department.
